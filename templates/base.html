About My Podcast

Create your own Podcast within seconds!
All you need is to enter the topic and the AI magic will create a fun podcast for you.
In 3 simple steps:
  1. First, register with a username and a password of your choice.
  2. Login with your username and password
  3. Enter the theme of your podcast
…and puff – in seconds, the magic of AI will have your full voice podcast ready: a podcast with dialogues between 2 or more people on the topic chosen by you! Any topic!
You can create as many Podcasts as you want, and next time you log in, all your podcasts will be there, ready for you to listen to them again!

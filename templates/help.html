Help!

Confused? We are here to help.
You can create a Podcast with the default settings, or you can customize its properties, via the Options page.
First of all, if you are not registered, you need to do so with a username and a password of your choice. This way, MyPodcast will remember all the podcasts you create and you will be able to listen to them again and again and again…
After you login with your username and password, you can create your podcast by just entering the topic you want it to cover.
But wait… There is more! You can customize your podcast. You can select the AI provider, the names and sexes of the podcast hosts, their voice, mood etc. To do so, just click (or touch) the menu icon and select the Options page from the menu.

On the Options page, you can select the AI provider, (currently OpenAI or Google Gemini) and then the sex and voice of the 2 hosts of your podcast. Each AI provider comes with a number of different voices, male and female, each one with its own characteristics.

Choose your favorite voices from the relevant drop-down menus and fill the mood fields with the vibe you prefer. Save your preferences and finally enter the theme of your podcast. It will take a couple of minutes for AI to do its magic creating your podcast. When it ready, it will start playing immediately.

You can create as many Podcasts as you want, and next time you log in, all your podcasts will be there, ready for you to listen to them again!
